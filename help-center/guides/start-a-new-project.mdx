## Create Your Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    On the Silly Smile dashboard, click the **Projects** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Project">
    Press the **“+ New Project”** button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set name & description">
    Enter your project name and (optionally) a short description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite team">
    Add teammates by email or copy the invite link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start documenting">
    Your new project is ready! Click **"New Page"** to create docs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
