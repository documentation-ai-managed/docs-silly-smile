title: How do I create a new documentation page?
description: Learn how to add docs to your Silly Smile space.
---

## Steps

1. Click the **"New Page"** button on your Silly Smile dashboard.
2. Enter a title, e.g. "Project Plan".
3. Add content using the editor (supports text, images, lists).
4. Click **Save**.

Your new document instantly appears in the sidebar. Edit anytime by clicking its name.

<Callout kind="tip">
Team members can also contribute by clicking **Edit** on any page.
</Callout>
