## Overview

Silly Smile provides a centralized space to organize, manage, and share your project documentation effortlessly. You create custom spaces for teams, embed docs anywhere, and collaborate in real-time. Start building your knowledge base today with intuitive tools that scale from small projects to enterprise needs.

</Callout>

## Get Started in Minutes

Follow these steps to set up your first Silly Smile space and publish your initial documentation page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.sillysmile.com` and create a free account using your email or GitHub. Verify your email to unlock full features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** from the dashboard. Name it (e.g., "Project Docs") and select a template like "API Reference" or "User Guide".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Page" icon="file-text">
    Navigate to your space and create a new page. Use the Markdown editor to add content, headings, and images.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish and Share" icon="share-2">
    Preview your page, then publish. Copy the shareable link or generate an embed code for external use.

````mdx
<iframe
  src="https://docs.sillysmile.com/embed/your-space-slug"
  width="100%"
  height="600"
  frameborder="0"
/>
````
  </Step>
</Steps>
